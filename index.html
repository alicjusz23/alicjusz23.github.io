    Geodäsie und Geoinformatik, Leibniz Universität Hannover

    During my second exchange in the Erasmus+ Programme, I experienced living in Germany as an entirely ordnung country. I improved my spoken language and even managed to pass my courses in their language. Moreover, I put my further steps in coding, starting with Java. Quite challenging time, but also satisfying.

  • 2014/2016

    Geodesy and Cartography, Warsaw University of Technology

    After my previous education, I decided to take up a professional path starting Geographical Information Systems speciality. During my studies, I got expert knowledge about SDI and INSPIRE directive. As my master's thesis The concept and development of the information service in the field of public order for the city of Chelm I have created a simple GIS web application. Building the geoportal gave me incredible pleasure and learned me a lot about spatial data structure and developing a project.

  • 2012

    Geomatics, Aalto University, Helsinki

    In Helsinki, I spent time as an exchange student of the LLP Erasmus Programme. I lived my happy 4-month adventure getting to know better how can I use GIS in different applications. I explored projects with the use of modern geospatial hardware in Photogrammetry and GIS and Geodetic Measurement courses, which were available at Aalto University. Meantime I was discovering the most beautiful places in Finland with friends from other corners of the world.

  • 2010/2014

    Geodesy and Cartography, Warsaw University of Technology

    During my bachelor's degree studies I enjoyed time getting to know surveying and geospatial principles. It was my first contact with GIS. The most fascinating task was my bachelor's thesis: Photogrammetric inventory of an architectural object, in which I was modelling two walls of a 3D object. I have scanned the object by myself with a terrestrial laser scanner, which was one of the most thrilling experience.
